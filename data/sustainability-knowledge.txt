Scope 1: Direct emissions from company-owned operations.
Scope 2: Indirect emissions from purchased electricity.
Scope 3: Emissions from suppliers, commuting, packaging, waste, shipping.

Switch to LED lighting → saves 50–70% energy.
Install motion sensors → 20–30% savings.
Turn off idle equipment → 5–10% savings.
Digitize receipts and forms → reduces paper cost.
Recycle segregated waste → lowers disposal fees.
Use recycled paper → reduces procurement footprint.
Install faucet aerators → reduces 30–40% water use.

Offices: optimize AC usage (24°C), prefer laptops over desktops.
Restaurants: reduce food waste, optimize refrigeration.
Manufacturing: use energy-efficient motors and preventive maintenance.
E-commerce: use sustainable packaging and optimize delivery routes.

30-day actions: energy audit, waste segregation system, basic reporting.
60-day actions: LED retrofitting, green procurement guidelines.
90-day actions: carbon footprint baseline, vendor sustainability checklists.
